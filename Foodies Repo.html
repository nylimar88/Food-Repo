Classic Chocolate Chip Cookies

The ultimate crispy-edged, chewy-centered comfort food.

Prep Time 15 mins
Cook Time 10 mins
Servings 24 cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons hot water
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat & Prep: Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F). Cream together the butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth.
  2. Mix Wet Ingredients: Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla extract.
  3. Add Leavening: Dissolve baking soda in hot water. Add to batter along with salt.
  4. Combine Dry Ingredients: Blend in flour and chocolate chips until just combined (do not overmix).
  5. Bake: Drop large spoonfuls of dough onto ungreased pans. Bake for about 10 minutes, or until the edges are nicely browned.
  6. Cool: Let them cool on the baking sheet for 2 minutes before transferring to a wire rack.
